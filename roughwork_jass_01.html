Jass

Film titles, poster & packaging

The aim of this project was to design the title sequence, poster and promotional material for Jass, a graduate film from the National Film School at IADT.
The dystopian narrative of the film revolves around humans wiping out animals in fear that they are becoming too intelligent. The irrational actions of the humans have led to technological regression. The visual language of the title sequence and promotional material reflect this in their mechanical, letterpressed style.
